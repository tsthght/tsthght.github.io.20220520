纪录片《外科医生是怎样炼成的》—— 神圣的大脑

2022/05/15 posted in  film

本集以一名脑外科教授的口吻娓娓道来。和国内的医疗题材的纪录片不同，这部纪录片不是重点在讨论“医患关系”、“救死扶伤”、“高超的技术”，而是通过一台手术，穿插叙述了一名来自贫困地区的孩子，以何种信念成长为如今的知名医学院的脑外科教授的。是更深层次东西的表达，比如克服困难的信念、执着，不服输的毅力、最自己的信任等等。

总之非常喜欢故事的叙述方式以及表达的东西。

以下是一些比较好的句子：

  • We had no idea where "faraway" was, but just looking at the railroad, they seemed to just go toward the horizon.
  • If I follow that trail one day, I'll get to faraway, and maybe one day I'll get to see things that no one else has seen before.
  • Now, when I'm in the oprerating room, I'm looking at the brain. This unexplored frontier that we understand so little.
  • I didn't have dreams of becoming a brain surgeon. I didn't event know what a brain surgeon was. And now, here I am at the Mayo Clinic as the Mayo Professor of Neurosurgery, potentially the best hospital in the world. And that sometimes makes me want to pinch myself.
  • My patients allow me to use my hands to be able to navigate their brains and to use my own brain to defeat their disease. That, to me, is the beauty of what I do.
  • He is a lovely patient. The future of his family is on his shoulders. We're going to have to do an operation because the tumor is growing again. I'am going to try to cure him.
  • He is definitely scared.
  • I always view any tumor as a foe that I need to defeat.
  • I left Mexico whe I was 19 years old. And now, 33 years later, I come back to Mexicali. This area remains hanging in time. as if was waiting for me to come back and say hi.
  • I do have vivid memories of my grandfather. My grandfather was not educated. He was not well-traveled, but he would always encourage me to go off to a little trail and just take the path that no one has taken before.
  • As a kid, I did have this extraordinary ability to figure out a way to move around obstacles.
  • Many times life will put you down on your knees. And the greatest challenge that we have is to get up and try again.
  • For years, I would avoid answering the question of where was I from. And to be honest with you, sometimes I would even lie about my roots.
  • I can't change the fact that I grew up poor, I can't change the fact that I came as a poor immigrant. I just embrace it and I work with it.
